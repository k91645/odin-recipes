Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna dish that can be made within 30 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 pound Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

Steps

  1. On a stove, cook sausage, beef, minced onion and garlic together
  2. Boil a large pot of lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes
